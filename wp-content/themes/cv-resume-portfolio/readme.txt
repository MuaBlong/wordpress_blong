== Description ==

CV Resume Portfolio is a clean, modern, and professional free WordPress theme designed for individuals who want to build a strong personal presence online. Ideal for job seekers, designers, developers, freelancers, consultants, and creative professionals, this theme offers a well-structured layout to showcase skills, work experience, education, achievements, and projects through a polished CV template, resume template, and portfolio template. Its lightweight and responsive design ensures fast loading, smooth navigation, and an excellent viewing experience across desktops, tablets, and smartphones. With easy customization options for colors, typography, and content, users can align the design with their personal branding without any coding knowledge. The theme includes dedicated sections such as About Me, Education, Experience, Skills, Portfolio, and Contact Information, making it perfect for creating a professional resume, creative CV, modern portfolio, or online portfolio. Built with an SEO-friendly structure for better search visibility, it also supports drag-and-drop editing, clean formatting, and compatibility with popular plugins like Contact Form 7 for seamless communication. CV Resume Portfolio is an excellent choice for anyone aiming to stand out in a competitive job market or establish a credible online identity with a visually appealing and professional digital resume.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
